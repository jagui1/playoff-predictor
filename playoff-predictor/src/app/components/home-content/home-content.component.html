What can I do next?

Create a profile to make picks

Login using Auth0 in the top right corner to create your profile that will store your picks. Once you login you can edit your name on the profile page in the dropdown in the top right.

Make Your Picks

Once you have created your account using your phone number you'll be able to save your picks to your account. Pick the winner of each game in the playoffs for one point each along with the superbowl prediction and winner for bonus points.

View Schedule

Check out the current bracket of the remaining teams in the playoffs.

View Scoreboard

Scoreboard is the live view of everyone who has submitted picks and how many points they have earned so far.
